## opus_client.py — _call streaming check

  src/theory_copilot/opus_client.py:44
    response = self.client.messages.create(
        model=self.model,
        max_tokens=32000,
        thinking={"type": "adaptive", "display": "summarized"},

  PROBLEM: nonstreaming `messages.create()` + max_tokens=32000 + adaptive
  thinking exceeds Anthropic SDK's 10-minute nonstreaming guard. The SDK
  raises ValueError before any network call.

  This is the ROOT CAUSE of Q3 + Q4 `make demo` failures.

  results/live_evidence/api_validation_log.txt Part 5 documents the fix
  was applied on 2026-04-22 (switch to client.messages.stream(...) +
  get_final_message()), but the HEAD `opus_client.py:_call` still uses
  the broken nonstreaming shape.
