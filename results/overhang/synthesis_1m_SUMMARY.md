## 5 invariant conditions (sufficient conditions for gate passage)

Derived empirically by Opus from the 74-rejection failure distribution:

1. **Pathway pair requirement.** Compound MUST contain ≥1 Proliferation
   marker (TOP2A/MKI67/CDK1/CCNB1/PCNA) AND ≥1 HIF/ccA marker in OPPOSITE
   SIGN. *Evidence: all 9 survivors have this form; every single-axis
   skeleton failed delta_baseline with Δ ≤ 0.03.*

2. **Non-monotonicity of single gene.** Compound MUST NOT reduce to a
   monotone transform of a single high-baseline gene. Rank correlation
   > 0.98 with any single input → cannot clear +0.05. *Evidence: 23
   CA9-alone variants achieved AUROC 0.965 but Δbaseline = 0.000.*

3. **Transform depth ≤ 2.** Compound MUST preserve dynamic range —
   triple/quadruple log1p stacks collapse to AUROC ≈ 0.50. *Evidence:
   all 14 log1p(log1p(log1p(·))) forms in tier2 produced AUROC=0.500.*

4. **Sign orientation.** Compound MUST be ccB-high minus ccA-high (not
   reversed). Sign-flipped forms produce AUROC < 0.5 and fail decoy_p.
   *Evidence: `exp(exp(x1*(x3-x9))*…)` yielded AUROC 0.021 (Δ = −0.945).*

5. **Low-correlation constituent axes.** Two constituent axes must have
   |r| < 0.7 in the cohort; otherwise Δconfound < 0.03 collapses.
   *Evidence: multi-variable forms collapsing onto covariate-correlated
   single axis failed both Δbaseline and Δconfound.*

## Synthesis insight (Opus-authored)

> "The campaign reveals a sharp **bimodal failure landscape**: 27 rejections
> collapse to AUROC ≈ 0.5 from log1p-stack saturation (pure mathematical
> pathology), while 23 rejections plateau at AUROC 0.96-0.99 but fail
> delta_baseline because they are monotone functions of CA9 alone. Only
> the Proliferation − HIF-2α contrast survives, clearing the +0.05
> delta_baseline hurdle by combining two biologically orthogonal ccRCC

> is effectively a trap: any law that fails to cross into the Proliferation
> × HIF-2α boundary cannot advance, regardless of mathematical complexity."

## Why this was not possible 6 months ago

- 1M context at standard pricing (Opus 4.7 release) enables loading the
  full 83-point experimental corpus + 5 paper abstracts + methodology
  in a single prompt. Pre-4.7, RAG chunking would have forced
  within-cluster reasoning only, missing the cross-cluster bimodal
  failure pattern.
- Adaptive extended thinking (`type: adaptive, effort: high`) gave
  ~50 s of internal reasoning to synthesize five skeletons + five
  invariant conditions + one integrative summary in one output.

## Consistency with Lane I · I2 Rashomon analysis

Opus's invariant condition #1 ("must contain proliferation marker +
HIF marker in opposite sign") was independently confirmed by empirical
enumeration of all 990 2-gene pairs: **15 of top 20 pairs contain a
proliferation-axis gene**, all of top 5 pairs have `proliferation − X`
form. The two approaches — LLM-1M-context synthesis and exhaustive
990-pair enumeration — converge on the same structural sufficient
condition.
